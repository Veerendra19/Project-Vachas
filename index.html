About Vachas

Let the world hear you!

Vachas: The Literary Club of KL University is a vibrant community that seeks to ignite a love for literature and creative expression among its members. Through a variety of engaging activities and events, Vachas aims to cultivate the skills of speaking, writing, and designing in its participants. From lively discussions on thought-provoking books and hosting guest speakers for various occasions to organizing captivating writing competitions, and workshops and designing visually stunning literary projects, Vachas provides an atmosphere of intellectual stimulation and artistic exploration. Anchoring all its activities is the belief that literature has the power to inspire, educate, and transform individuals. For someone who is passionate about literature and wants to hone their skills in communication, writing, and design, Vachas is the perfect avenue to explore and enhance your college experience with countless memories to rely on your tough days but also empower you with essential skills that will benefit you in your academic and professional journey.

Our Wings

Writing

Writing is a beautiful art that transcends time and space, allowing thoughts and emotions to take shape through the power of words.With every stroke of the pen or tap of the keyboard, we embark on a journey of creation, weaving together thoughts, ideas, and emotions to create something meaningful and lasting. It is a powerful tool that allows us to leave a mark on the world, to inspire, to provoke thought, and to evoke change.

Speaking

Speaking is a fundamental form of human communication, enabling us to convey our thoughts, emotions, and ideas to others. Through spoken words, we express our identities, share stories, and engage in meaningful conversations. It is a dynamic process that involves not only the words we choose but also our tone, body language, and presence. Our ability to speak effectively has a profound impact on how we connect and influence the world around us.

Designing

Designing is a creative process that transforms ideas and concepts into tangible forms. It is a harmonious blend of artistry, functionality, and innovation, where aesthetics meet practicality. It involves careful consideration of color, shape, texture, and composition to create visually appealing and purposeful outcomes. Through design, we have the power to shape our surroundings, influence emotions, and leave a lasting impression.
